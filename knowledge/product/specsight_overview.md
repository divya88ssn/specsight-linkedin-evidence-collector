# Specsight Overview

## Vision

Enable organizations to continuously understand, validate, and communicate how their product actually behaves.

Rather than relying on static requirements or manually maintained documentation, Specsight derives a living specification from the implementation and continuously keeps it synchronized as the product evolves.

---

## Product Philosophy

Traditional documentation captures intended behavior.

Specsight captures observed product behavior and makes it understandable across Product, Engineering, QA, Customer Success and Leadership.

The product is designed to reduce documentation drift and improve shared product understanding.

---

## Core Concepts

- Living Specification

- Product Knowledge

- Continuous Synchronization

## What Specsight Does

- Creates a living product specification
- Organizes product behavior into Features and Scenarios
- Answers product questions
- Tracks behavioral evolution
- Explains relationships between features
- Summarizes product changes
- Keeps documentation synchronized with releases
- Exports product knowledge

## What Specsight Does NOT Claim

- Roadmapping
- Sprint planning
- Test execution
- Release approvals
- Analytics platform
- Production monitoring
- CI/CD orchestration
