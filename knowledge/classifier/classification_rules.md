# Classification Rules

## Evidence First

Use only evidence contained in the LinkedIn post.

Do not invent organizational context.

---

## Workflow

Identify one primary workflow stage.

Allow multiple secondary stages.

---

## Failure Modes

Identify one or more observable failure modes.

---

## Capabilities

Map to one primary Specsight capability.

Allow multiple supporting capabilities.

---

## Fit

Use one of:

- Direct
- Indirect
- Adjacent
- No Fit

Never force a Specsight fit.

---

## Confidence

High

Medium

Low

Lower confidence when evidence is weak.

---

## Quotes

Extract supporting text from the post.

Never paraphrase evidence.

---

## Reasoning

Explain why the selected capability addresses the observed organizational problem.

Reason only from documented Specsight capabilities.
